# 110-Second Demo Script

## 0–15 seconds — Problem

“Companies have plenty of documents, but raw data is not automatically safe context for an AI agent. It can be stale, contradictory, unapproved, or prohibited by policy.”

Show the Company Brain overview.

## 15–35 seconds — Evidence and governed truth

Open a source document and its evidence panel.

“Company Memory preserves source evidence, provenance, chunks, and freshness. Company Brain separately stores governed claims, entities, metrics, and policies.”

## 35–55 seconds — Point-in-time context

Open a context packet.

“For each mission, the compiler persists approved facts, policy decisions, gaps, stale context, and contradictions as a point-in-time packet. Later source changes cannot silently rewrite the context used for this decision.”

## 55–80 seconds — Human authority and learning

Open a learning approval packet.

“A result can propose a new organizational claim, but it cannot write itself into the Brain. The packet shows evidence, conflicts, and policy. Approval authorizes a separate, idempotent writeback.”

## 80–100 seconds — Production controls

Show capabilities and observability.

“Mutation, learning writeback, and source sync have independent kill switches. Operators can inspect health, pending approvals, stale sources, backup readiness, and rollback evidence without exposing customer content in metrics.”

## 100–110 seconds — Close

“Amethyst demonstrates how I approach applied AI: models reason, governed systems decide, and production claims remain bound to evidence.”
